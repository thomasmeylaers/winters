ABOUT US

35 years of craftsmanship

Winters is a team of 10 people which focusses on designing, creating and maintaining high-quality injection molds for diverse sectors and techniques.

We also offer the manufacturing of precision parts for machines and tools.

OUR PROCESS

Development

Based on a product model, material and machine data received from the customer, we start a feasibility study and design the mould with the desired uniformity (1,2,4,8,16,...).

Construction

After approval of the design, our team manufactures the mould or part using CAD/CAM (cimatron) and our machines.

Testing

Finally, we can also test the mould on one of our injection moulding machines with a clamping force of 25, 50 or 100 tonnes.
